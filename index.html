Mathematical Model: Logistic Growth

Traffic growth is modeled using an S-Curve, where demand \( D(t) \) at year \( t \) approaches maximum market capacity \( K \):
\( D(t) = \frac{K}{1 + \left(\frac{K - D_0}{D_0}\right) e^{-rt}} \)
The Inflection Point (\( D = K/2 \)) marks where the second derivative turns negative, indicating the transition from accelerating adoption to market saturation.
